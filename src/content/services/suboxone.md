Dr. Zaidi is certified to perform outpatient narcotics detoxification and maintenance treatment using buprenorphine. This service is for adults dealing with opioid use disorder — whether the source is prescription pain medication, heroin, or other narcotics.

### How Suboxone works

Suboxone® is an FDA-approved, sublingual film that contains buprenorphine — a partial opioid antagonist that eliminates cravings without producing the harmful side effects associated with methadone. For most patients, it allows you to function normally at work, with family, and in everyday life while you focus on recovery.

### Treatment is more than medication

Suboxone is one part of a recovery plan. Dr. Zaidi pairs medication with the psychological work needed to address the underlying causes of addiction — because if you don't treat the cause, the cycle continues. The practice is confidential and judgment-free, and the goal is steady, sustainable recovery.
