Medication management is the foundation of most psychiatric care, and getting it right takes time. Dr. Zaidi's approach is to start carefully, monitor closely, and adjust based on how you're actually responding — not on a one-size-fits-all formula.

### What you can expect

- **Initial evaluation:** A thorough conversation about your history, current symptoms, and any past medications
- **Personalized plan:** A treatment plan designed around your goals, lifestyle, and tolerance for side effects
- **Regular follow-up:** Scheduled check-ins to see what's working, what isn't, and what to adjust
- **In-person or telepsychiatry:** Visits available either way, with most ongoing medication management possible by video

If you've felt rushed by past psychiatrists, or like your medications were prescribed without a real plan, this is meant to be different.
