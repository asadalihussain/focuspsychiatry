Spravato® (esketamine) is an FDA-approved nasal spray for adults with treatment-resistant depression — depression that hasn't responded adequately to at least two traditional antidepressants. It's also approved as an adjunctive treatment for depressive symptoms in adults with major depressive disorder who have acute suicidal ideation or behavior.

### What to know about Spravato

- **In-office only:** Spravato must be administered in a certified medical office under supervision
- **Monitoring period:** Patients are monitored for about two hours after each dose
- **Schedule:** Initial treatment is twice weekly for the first month, then tapers down
- **Used with an oral antidepressant:** Spravato is given alongside an ongoing oral antidepressant, not as a replacement

If you've tried multiple antidepressants without finding relief, Spravato may be an option worth discussing. Reach out to Focus Psychiatry to see if it's appropriate for your situation.
