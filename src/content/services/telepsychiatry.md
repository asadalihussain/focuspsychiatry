Focus Psychiatry offers secure telepsychiatry visits for patients located anywhere in Texas. The format works well for most kinds of ongoing care — medication management, follow-up appointments, therapy check-ins, and many initial consultations.

### When telepsych works well

- Follow-up medication management visits
- Patients living more than 30 minutes from Alvin
- Anyone with mobility, transportation, or scheduling constraints
- Busy schedules where an in-person visit isn't practical

### When in-person is better

Some situations are better handled in the office — first visits for complex presentations, addiction medicine including Suboxone induction, and any treatment that requires in-office administration (such as Spravato).

We'll always let you know which format makes the most sense for your situation.
