Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is an FDA-approved, non-invasive treatment for major depression that hasn't responded to medication. It uses targeted magnetic pulses to stimulate the regions of the brain associated with mood regulation.

### What TMS treatment looks like

- **Non-invasive:** No anesthesia, no sedation, no IV
- **No systemic side effects:** Unlike medication, TMS doesn't affect the whole body
- **Outpatient:** Sessions take roughly 20–40 minutes; you drive yourself home
- **Course:** A typical course runs daily for several weeks, then tapers

TMS is most often considered when multiple antidepressants haven't worked, or when side effects from medication have been intolerable. If you're curious whether TMS could be right for you, schedule a consultation with Dr. Zaidi.
