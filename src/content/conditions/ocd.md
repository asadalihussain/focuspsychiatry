OCD is more than being "particular" or "organized." It's intrusive thoughts you can't let go of, and behaviors you feel compelled to repeat in order to bring those thoughts down. Treatment combines medication with structured therapy techniques aimed at reducing both the obsessions and the compulsions over time.
