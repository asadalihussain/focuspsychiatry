Depression is more than feeling sad. It can affect how you sleep, eat, work, and relate to the people around you. Dr. Zaidi treats depression at every level of severity — from low mood that has lingered for weeks to treatment-resistant depression that hasn't responded to multiple medications.

### How we approach treatment

Your first visit is a thorough evaluation: medical history, current symptoms, prior medications, and a conversation about what's been working and what hasn't. From there we build a plan that may include:

- Medication management with regular follow-up
- Integrative therapy techniques alongside medication
- Referral to in-network therapy partners when ongoing counseling is needed
- For severe or treatment-resistant cases, in-office options including Spravato

You don't have to commit to anything on day one. The goal of the first appointment is to understand what's happening and lay out a plan you feel comfortable with.
