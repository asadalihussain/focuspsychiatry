Anxiety shows up in many forms — racing thoughts, panic attacks, avoidance, difficulty sleeping, or a constant low hum of dread that won't quiet down. Focus Psychiatry treats the full spectrum of anxiety disorders for ages 14 and up.

### What treatment looks like

We start by understanding the pattern: when it shows up, what triggers it, and how it interferes with your day. Treatment may include medication, integrative therapy techniques, lifestyle interventions, or a combination. Many patients see meaningful change within the first few weeks of starting a plan.
