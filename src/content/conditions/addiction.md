Addiction to alcohol and drugs is not the problem. It is only the symptom of the problem — the way one chooses to cope with the core issue. Addiction is a road sign that life has become unmanageable. You can detox from drugs and alcohol, but that only addresses the symptom. If the core reason isn't addressed, it will continue to cause pain and start over again.

At Focus Psychiatry, we treat the underlying psychological and physiological causes of your addiction. We don't believe one approach works for everyone. We design treatment modalities that fit you and your loved ones — a custom plan that's just right for you.

### About Suboxone

Suboxone® is a safer, more effective alternative to Methadone, used to completely eliminate cravings associated with typical opiate addiction (heroin, pain pills, etc.). Many similar medications are used, including Bunavail and Zubsolv. Suboxone® accomplishes the same goals as Methadone without many of the harmful side effects, and is an FDA-approved medication administered as a sublingual (under-the-tongue) film.

Buprenorphine, the active ingredient in Suboxone®, is a partial antagonist with only limited opioid effects. It displaces existing opioids and prevents others from attaching to chemical receptors in the brain.
