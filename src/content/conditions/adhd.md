ADHD shows up differently at different ages. In adolescents it might be missed homework, lost belongings, or a teacher's note. In adults it's more often missed deadlines, scattered thinking, and a sense that you're working twice as hard for half the output.

Focus Psychiatry evaluates and treats ADHD for ages 14 and up. Treatment typically involves medication management paired with practical strategies to help you build sustainable focus and organization habits.
